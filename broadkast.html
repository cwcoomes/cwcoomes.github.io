BroadKast!

Location-based social media mobile application

Project Summary

BroadKast! is a location based social media mobile application developed as my senior project at University of Houston in 2017. The goal of BroadKast! was to get people to stop socializing through their phones, instead enouraging physical interactions and meetups. By creating a "Kast", a user could display a local event on the map in which other nearby users could see and participate based on the specified privacy settings. Example events include Study Groups, Meet & Greets, Role Playing, etc.
